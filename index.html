Frequently Asked Questions

How does PicShine AI work?

PicShine AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze and enhance your photos. All processing happens in your browser for maximum privacy and speed. Our AI models are trained on millions of high-quality images to deliver professional results.

Is PicShine AI really free?

Yes! PicShine AI offers a completely free tier with unlimited basic photo enhancement, background removal, and batch processing up to 10 images. No registration required, no watermarks, and no hidden fees.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and even RAW files. Maximum file size is 50MB per image for free users and 200MB for Pro users.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy. We don't store, view, or have access to any of your images.

Can I use enhanced images commercially?

Yes! All enhanced images are yours to use however you like, including for commercial purposes. You retain full ownership and rights to your processed images.

How fast is the processing?

Processing speed depends on your device's capabilities and image size. Typically, basic enhancements take 2-5 seconds, while advanced AI features like super resolution may take 10-30 seconds per image.
