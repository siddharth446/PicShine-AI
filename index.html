Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PicShine AI and learn how to get the most out of our photo enhancement platform.

How does PicShine AI work?

PicShine AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms and neural networks to analyze and enhance your photos. All processing happens directly in your browser using WebGL and WebAssembly for maximum privacy and speed.

Our AI models have been trained on millions of high-quality images to understand photo composition, lighting, and aesthetic principles, allowing them to make intelligent enhancements automatically.

Is PicShine AI really free to use?

Yes! PicShine AI is completely free to use with no hidden costs, subscription fees, or usage limits. You can enhance unlimited photos without any restrictions.

We believe that powerful photo enhancement tools should be accessible to everyone, which is why we've made our platform completely free and will continue to do so.

What image formats and sizes are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF. Maximum file size is 10MB per image, and you can process multiple images simultaneously.

For optimal results, we recommend using high-resolution images (at least 1080p) as they provide more detail for our AI algorithms to work with.

How secure and private is my data?

Your privacy is our top priority. All image processing happens locally in your browser - your photos are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere online.

This means your images remain completely private and secure on your device throughout the entire enhancement process. We have no access to your photos at any time.

Can I use enhanced images for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! All enhanced images are yours to use however you like, including for commercial purposes. There are no licensing restrictions or royalties on the enhanced results.

Whether you're a professional photographer, content creator, or business owner, you have full rights to use your enhanced images for any purpose.

What browsers and devices does PicShine AI support?

PicShine AI works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It's fully responsive and works on desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones.

For the best performance, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox on a device with at least 4GB of RAM for smooth processing of high-resolution images.
